# GameDB — Your Personal Gaming Journal

## What is it?

GameDB is a free web app for tracking every game you've ever played, are currently playing, or want to play. Think of it as Goodreads, but for video games — with a vibe.

## The problem

You've played hundreds of games across Steam, PlayStation, Xbox, and Switch. But you can't remember what you rated that RPG from 2019, how many hours you sank into Elden Ring, or which games your friend keeps telling you to try. Your gaming history is scattered across platforms with no single place to see it all.

## The solution

GameDB brings your entire gaming life into one place.

## Key Features

**Import your library in seconds** — Connect Steam or Xbox and import your full game library with one click. No manual entry needed. (PlayStation coming soon.)

**Track everything** — Status (Playing, Completed, On Hold, Dropped, Plan to Play), 10-star ratings, hours played, personal notes, and cover art for every game.

**Listening Room** — Every game page has an embedded Spotify player loaded with that game's soundtrack. Hit play and browse your collection with the music setting the mood. Choose from ambient themes like "Silent Hill Vibes" or "Chill RPG" for persistent background music across the whole site.

**Blend with friends** — Share a 6-digit code with a friend to connect. View each other's libraries and compare side-by-side: games in common, games only you have, who played more hours, and a "rating agreement" score that shows how similar your tastes are.

**Dashboard & Stats** — See your gaming story at a glance: total hours, completion rate, favorite platform, highest-rated game, and fun insights like "your 340 hours of gaming equals watching Lord of the Rings 30 times."

**Quick add from anywhere** — The search bar finds games from the RAWG database. Tap + and the title, image, platform, and genres are pre-filled. Two clicks to add any game ever made.

## Who is it for?

Gamers who play across multiple platforms and want one place to remember, rate, and share their gaming journey. Whether you have 20 games or 500.
